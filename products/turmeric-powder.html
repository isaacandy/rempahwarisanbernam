The Golden Spice

Our Serbuk Kunyit Asli is the essence of Malaysian home cooking. Made from 100% pure, high-quality turmeric root, it provides a vibrant golden color and a warm, earthy, and slightly bitter flavor. It's the essential ingredient for 'masak lemak', marinades for fried chicken ('ayam goreng kunyit'), and is celebrated for its natural anti-inflammatory properties.
