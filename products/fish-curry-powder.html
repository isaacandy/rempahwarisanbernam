Spice Blend

Serbuk Kari Ikan Asli

Bring coastal Malaysian flavors to your table with our authentic fish curry blend. It enhances fish, prawns, and squid with a signature tangy profile, perfect with tamarind.

Common Uses

  • Classic Malaysian fish head curry.
  • Flavorful curries with fish, prawns, or squid.
  • Spicy and tangy seafood stews ('Asam Pedas' style).
  • Marinade for grilled or fried fish.

Key Ingredients

Coriander, Cumin, Dried Chilli, Turmeric, Black Pepper, Fennel, Fenugreek.

The Taste of the Coast

Our Serbuk Kari Ikan Asli is a tribute to Malaysia's rich coastal culinary heritage. This blend is specifically formulated to complement the delicate flavors of seafood. It has a signature tangy and aromatic profile, achieved through a unique combination of spices including fennel and fenugreek, designed to be perfectly balanced when cooked with tamarind juice.
