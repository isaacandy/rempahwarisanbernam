Our Chronology

Half a Century of
Flavor Wisdom.

The Rempah Warisan story began in 1972, in a small wooden workshop nestled in the heart of Bernam. What started as a local tradition of hand-roasting whole spices soon became the benchmark for purity in the region. For over 50 years, we have preserved the same secret proportions that earned us the trust of Malaysia's most legendary culinary houses.

The Dawn Roast

"Since the 70s, our master roasters believe spices should only meet the heat at dawn, when the air is crisp, ensuring the oils never turn bitter."

The Golden Ratio

"The proportion of our Briyani blend was finalized on a rainy night in 1975 and has remained unchanged for five decades."

Operations & SOP

Production Workflow

01
Coarse Crush

Whole spices are broken into uniform granules using industrial breakers.

02
ACM Milling

Air-classified milling (80-450 mesh) for ultra-fine consistency.

03
Sieving

Mechanical vibration screening through precision seivers.

04
Mixing

Homogeneous blending in high-grade 304 stainless steel mixers.

05
Packaging

Hygienic retail & bulk packing under strict quality control.
